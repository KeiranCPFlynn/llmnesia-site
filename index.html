Local-first. Private by design.

  • Stored locally in your browser
  • No external servers
  • No cloud sync by default
  • You control export and deletion

Permissions explained: Storage — saves your index and settings locally in your browser. Tabs — navigates you to the right conversation when you open a search result. Active tab — detects which AI platform you are currently visiting. Scripting — injects the search palette and index indicator into AI chat pages. No permission is used to read data from any site other than the AI platforms listed above.

FAQ

Does this send my chats to a server?

No. LLMnesia is local-first and keeps indexed data in your browser storage.

Which sites are supported?

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, DeepSeek, Grok, Mistral, Kimi, Qwen, and Google AI Studio.

Will it slow down my browser?

Indexing is designed to be lightweight and incremental. Most users should see minimal impact.

Can I delete everything?

Yes. You can clear indexed data locally and control export and deletion behavior.

Is there a paid plan?

LLMnesia is currently free while early. If pricing changes later, early users receive best access and discounts.
